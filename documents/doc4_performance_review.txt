DOCUMENT 4 — Performance Review Process
=====================================
Annual Performance Review (APR) Guidelines

REVIEW CYCLE
Performance reviews happen twice a year: mid-year (June) and year-end (December).
New joiners are reviewed at the 6-month mark.

RATINGS SCALE
5 — Exceptional: Consistently exceeds all targets
4 — Exceeds Expectations: Frequently surpasses goals
3 — Meets Expectations: Achieves all set goals
2 — Needs Improvement: Partially achieves goals
1 — Unsatisfactory: Fails to meet basic expectations

PROMOTION CRITERIA
Employees must score 4 or above in two consecutive reviews to be eligible for promotion.
Promotions are effective from the beginning of the next financial year.
Salary revision range: 8-20% for promotions, 3-8% for merit increments.

FEEDBACK PROCESS
360-degree feedback is collected from peers, subordinates, and managers.
Employees must complete self-evaluation 1 week before the review date.
