DOCUMENT 1 — HR Leave Policy
=====================================
Leave & Allowance Policy (Effective FY 2024-25)

1. ANNUAL LEAVE
All full-time employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per year.
Part-time employees receive leave on a pro-rata basis.
Unused leave can be carried forward up to 10 days to the next calendar year.

2. SICK LEAVE
Employees are entitled to 12 days of sick leave per year.
A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.

3. LEAVE TRAVEL ALLOWANCE (LTA)
LTA is available to all permanent employees who have completed at least 1 year of service.
Employees can claim LTA twice in a block of 4 years.
The maximum LTA claimable is 1 month's basic salary per trip.
LTA covers travel expenses for self and immediate family (spouse + up to 2 children).
Claims must be submitted within 3 months of travel completion.
Only domestic travel within the country qualifies for LTA.

4. MATERNITY / PATERNITY LEAVE
Maternity leave: 26 weeks for the first two children, 12 weeks for third child onward.
Paternity leave: 5 days for male employees within 6 months of child's birth.

5. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
3 days of paid bereavement leave for immediate family members.
1 day for extended family members.


DOCUMENT 2 — Remote Work Policy
=====================================
Work From Home (WFH) Guidelines (Updated: Jan 2024)

ELIGIBILITY
Employees who have completed 6 months of probation are eligible for remote work.
All remote work arrangements must be approved by the direct manager.

WFH LIMITS
Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week.
Roles marked as "on-site required" are not eligible for WFH.

EQUIPMENT & CONNECTIVITY
The company provides a one-time stipend of $500 for home office setup.
Employees are responsible for maintaining reliable internet connectivity.
VPN usage is mandatory when accessing company resources remotely.

PERFORMANCE
Remote employees are held to the same performance standards as on-site employees.
A 3-month WFH review is conducted by the manager.


DOCUMENT 3 — Employee Benefits Overview
=====================================
Health & Wellness Benefits (FY 2024)

HEALTH INSURANCE
All employees receive group health insurance from Day 1 of joining.
Coverage includes: hospitalization, outpatient, dental (basic), vision (annual checkup).
Sum insured: $50,000 per employee, $80,000 for family floater.
Pre-existing conditions covered after a 2-year waiting period.

MENTAL HEALTH SUPPORT
Free access to 6 counseling sessions per year via our EAP (Employee Assistance Program).
Mental health days: 2 additional paid days per year for mental wellness.

RETIREMENT / PROVIDENT FUND
Company contributes 12% of basic salary to the provident fund.
Employee contribution: minimum 12% of basic salary.
Vesting period: 5 years for full employer contribution vesting.

WELLNESS ALLOWANCE
$300 annual wellness allowance (gym, yoga, meditation apps, etc.).
Claims submitted through the HR portal by March 31 each year.


DOCUMENT 4 — Performance Review Process
=====================================
Annual Performance Review (APR) Guidelines

REVIEW CYCLE
Performance reviews happen twice a year: mid-year (June) and year-end (December).
New joiners are reviewed at the 6-month mark.

RATINGS SCALE
5 — Exceptional: Consistently exceeds all targets
4 — Exceeds Expectations: Frequently surpasses goals
3 — Meets Expectations: Achieves all set goals
2 — Needs Improvement: Partially achieves goals
1 — Unsatisfactory: Fails to meet basic expectations

PROMOTION CRITERIA
Employees must score 4 or above in two consecutive reviews to be eligible for promotion.
Promotions are effective from the beginning of the next financial year.
Salary revision range: 8–20% for promotions, 3–8% for merit increments.

FEEDBACK PROCESS
360-degree feedback is collected from peers, subordinates, and managers.
Employees must complete self-evaluation 1 week before the review date.


DOCUMENT 5 — Company Expense Policy
=====================================
Business Expense Reimbursement Policy

TRAVEL EXPENSES
Domestic flights: Economy class only.
International flights: Business class for flights > 8 hours.
Hotel: Up to $150/night domestic, $250/night international.
Meal per diem: $40/day domestic, $75/day international.

APPROVAL WORKFLOW
Expenses under $500: Direct manager approval.
Expenses $500–$2000: Manager + Finance team approval.
Expenses above $2000: CFO approval required.

SUBMISSION DEADLINE
All expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of incurring the expense.
Late submissions require VP-level approval.

PROHIBITED EXPENSES
Personal entertainment, alcohol purchases, luxury hotels, and first-class upgrades
are not reimbursable unless specifically pre-approved by the C-suite.


DOCUMENT 6 — Onboarding Process
=====================================
New Employee Onboarding (2024 Edition)

WEEK 1 — ORIENTATION
Day 1: IT setup, access credentials, welcome session with HR.
Day 2–3: Introduction to team, product/service walkthrough.
Day 4–5: Compliance training (mandatory — Data Privacy, Code of Conduct).

MONTH 1 GOALS
Complete mandatory e-learning modules (10 modules in total).
Set 90-day goals with manager.
Shadow at least 3 customer-facing calls (for client-facing roles).

BUDDY PROGRAM
Each new joiner is assigned a buddy from their team.
Buddy check-ins happen weekly for the first 30 days, then monthly for 90 days.

PROBATION PERIOD
Standard probation: 6 months.
Evaluation at 3 months and 6 months.
Successful completion leads to confirmation and eligibility for all benefits.


DOCUMENT 7 — Data Privacy Policy
=====================================
Data Privacy & Security Guidelines

PERSONAL DATA HANDLING
Employees must not share customer data externally without explicit authorization.
All personal data processed must comply with GDPR (for EU customers) and local laws.
Data retention: Customer data is kept for 7 years post-contract, then deleted.

DEVICE & ACCESS SECURITY
Strong passwords required (minimum 12 characters, alphanumeric + symbol).
Two-factor authentication (2FA) is mandatory for all company accounts.
Employees must not use personal devices for accessing confidential company data.

BREACH PROTOCOL
Any suspected data breach must be reported to the IT Security team within 1 hour.
The DPO (Data Protection Officer) must be notified within 24 hours.
External reporting to regulators required within 72 hours for confirmed breaches.

TRAINING
Annual data privacy training is mandatory for all employees.
Employees handling sensitive data undergo quarterly refresher training.
