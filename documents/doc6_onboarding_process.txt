DOCUMENT 6 — Onboarding Process
=====================================
New Employee Onboarding (2024 Edition)

WEEK 1 — ORIENTATION
Day 1: IT setup, access credentials, welcome session with HR.
Day 2-3: Introduction to team, product/service walkthrough.
Day 4-5: Compliance training (mandatory — Data Privacy, Code of Conduct).

MONTH 1 GOALS
Complete mandatory e-learning modules (10 modules in total).
Set 90-day goals with manager.
Shadow at least 3 customer-facing calls (for client-facing roles).

BUDDY PROGRAM
Each new joiner is assigned a buddy from their team.
Buddy check-ins happen weekly for the first 30 days, then monthly for 90 days.

PROBATION PERIOD
Standard probation: 6 months.
Evaluation at 3 months and 6 months.
Successful completion leads to confirmation and eligibility for all benefits.
