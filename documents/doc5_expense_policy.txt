Business Expense Reimbursement Policy

TRAVEL EXPENSES
Domestic flights: Economy class only.
International flights: Business class for flights > 8 hours.
Hotel: Up to $150/night domestic, $250/night international.
Meal per diem: $40/day domestic, $75/day international.

APPROVAL WORKFLOW
Expenses under $500: Direct manager approval.
Expenses $500-$2000: Manager + Finance team approval.
Expenses above $2000: CFO approval required.

SUBMISSION DEADLINE
All expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of incurring the expense.
Late submissions require VP-level approval.

PROHIBITED EXPENSES
Personal entertainment, alcohol purchases, luxury hotels, and first-class upgrades
are not reimbursable unless specifically pre-approved by the C-suite.
