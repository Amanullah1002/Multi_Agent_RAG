Leave & Allowance Policy (Effective FY 2024-25)

1. ANNUAL LEAVE
All full-time employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per year.
Part-time employees receive leave on a pro-rata basis.
Unused leave can be carried forward up to 10 days to the next calendar year.

2. SICK LEAVE
Employees are entitled to 12 days of sick leave per year.
A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.

3. LEAVE TRAVEL ALLOWANCE (LTA)
LTA is available to all permanent employees who have completed at least 1 year of service.
Employees can claim LTA twice in a block of 4 years.
The maximum LTA claimable is 1 month's basic salary per trip.
LTA covers travel expenses for self and immediate family (spouse + up to 2 children).
Claims must be submitted within 3 months of travel completion.
Only domestic travel within the country qualifies for LTA.

4. MATERNITY / PATERNITY LEAVE
Maternity leave: 26 weeks for the first two children, 12 weeks for third child onward.
Paternity leave: 5 days for male employees within 6 months of child's birth.

5. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
3 days of paid bereavement leave for immediate family members.
1 day for extended family members.
